About

Senior Game Developer with over 7 years of experience in the mobile gaming industry, currently serving as Lead Game Developer at Mood Games OU. I have a proven track record in designing and developing hyper-casual, idle, and casual games from concept to release—always with a strong focus on delivering fun, intuitive, and high-retention gameplay. My development background is backed by a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a passion for building games people genuinely enjoy playing.

Experience

Mood Games OU

Lead Game Developer

November 2020 - Present

At Mood Games, I’ve led development on hyper-casual, idle, and hybrid-casual titles, collaborating with publishers like Rollic, Supersonic, and Lion Studios. I’ve worked on successful games such as Drift ASMR, Traffic Expert, Traffic Jam Fever, and Click to Life, focusing on polished gameplay and strong player retention. Earlier, I contributed to the hit title Queen Bee and developed in-house tools like Audio Manager, Ads Manager, and Menu Manager to streamline production.

Arcadian Lab

Senior Game Developer

September 2017 - November 2020

As a Senior Game Developer, I led a small team creating hyper-casual games with publishers like Voodoo, Supersonic, and Moonee, including Police Quest, which performed well and was self-published. I focused on refining gameplay, player retention, and overall feel while adapting mechanics using feedback and data. Earlier, as a Game Developer, I built multiple prototypes for Voodoo, working on gameplay systems, ad integration, and performance improvements in Unity.
